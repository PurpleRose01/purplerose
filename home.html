About the Team

We are a team of freelancers specializing in Graphic Design and Website Development. Our members have spent a great deal of time honing their skills and we are confident in our ability to tackle all sorts of projects. This fact is also represented by our projects showcase web pages: Websites, Graphic Design.

Entrusting your ideas to someone else is indeed a daunting task, which is why we aim to be as accessible as possible for everyday regular people. Our development and design process is flexible allowing the customer to shape their product as they see fit while we take care of the technical and practical side of things. We make a point of prioritising customer satisfaction above all else and only ask for payment once the customer is happy
